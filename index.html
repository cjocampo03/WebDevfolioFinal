About CJ

I am a dynamic individual who seamlessly bridges the worlds of art and technology. As a passionate dancer, I have spent years perfecting my craft, honing my movements, and expressing myself through the graceful language of dance.

Whether I am pirouetting on stage or grooving to the beat in a studio, dance has been my muse, instilling discipline, creativity, and a keen sense of rhythm in me. Simultaneously, I am a dedicated IT student with a fervent love for developing programs and websites. The thrill of coding and designing digital landscapes excites me as much as dance does.

Through this unique combination of passions, I bring an innovative perspective to both realms, infusing my technical projects with the artistry and precision of a dancer, and my dance performances with the logical thinking and problem-solving skills of an IT enthusiast. These dual passions have shaped me into a versatile and well-rounded individual who thrives on the beauty of movement and the elegance of code.
